About studio

Apex Art Studio

Walnut Grove Dr, Langley

Apex Art Studio aims to offer our students the best! We provide professional in-studio and online visual art instruction for students of all age categories. Students are grouped according to age. Small class sizes allow for plenty of individual attention. Our studio emphasizes development in drawing and painting skills. The knowledgeable and experienced instructors and wonderful learning environment help ensure that our students will learn and master the skills they require in today’s dynamic world of art.

We are passionate; We are fun; And most importantly, we’re here to help our students explore different mediums and find their creative path.

Founder

Olga Smirnova

Founder & Studio Head Instructor Master of Fine Arts in Visual Arts MFA Member, Federation of Canadian Artists FCA

“To live is to be inspired”!

Olga has been a professional artist for 30 years.

Her passion for art has been a great constant in her life. She likes to create and make art because it allows her to express herself in a very meaningful way.

Her personal projects consist of a multiplicity of works, often using different media, grouped around specific themes and meanings. During researching and production, new areas of interest spontaneously arise and inevitably lead to the next body of works to explore.

Olga has taught art since 1992. Her qualifications include a Master of Fine Arts with Honors from Kuban State University, and a Computer Graphic Design Diploma from Vancouver Community College.

Olga is creative as both an artist and teacher. She has developed a series of approaches in order to address instructing students of different age groups, through her many years as a professional artist and instructor.
